Our Story

Founded in 2023, Frankassy emerged from a passion for exquisite craftsmanship and timeless design. Our journey began with a single wristwatch collection and has since grown to encompass a wide range of premium accessories.

Each piece in our collection is meticulously crafted, combining traditional techniques with modern aesthetics. We partner with skilled artisans around the world to bring you accessories that stand the test of time.

- The Frankassy Team
